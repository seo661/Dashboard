Key Takeaways

  • Phone is the dominant lead channel — of 141 tracked calls, 37 are genuine estimate/quote/inspection requests. Almost all valuable calls trace back to Google Organic and Google Business Profile listings.
  • Most "missed" calls are noise, not lost revenue — the large majority of unanswered calls are GBP voicemail spam and Google-listing scam calls. Use the Missed Calls table to focus only on the handful tagged Lead or Customer.
  • Attic insulation and spray foam lead service demand — see the Service Interest panel; the bulk of identified jobs are Denver-metro.
  • The Search-LU-Denver campaign is paused. It produced 10 conversions at $961 each. "home insulation services" and "insulation removal" are the keywords actually converting; "energy saving insulation" spent $858 with none.
